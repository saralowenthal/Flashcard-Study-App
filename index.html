Dadabase: The Ultimate Dad Joke Collection

Welcome to Dadabase, where you can study and enjoy a collection of dad jokes! This app lets you flip through digital flashcards to reveal the punchline to each joke. You can also mark jokes you already know, track your progress, and keep having fun while learning some classic dad humor.

Main Features:

  • Flip a Card: Click a card to reveal the punchline with a smooth flip animation.
  • Randomized Deck: The deck shuffles at the start to keep the session fresh.
  • Mark Known Jokes: Mark jokes you already know as "Known" to remove them from the deck.
  • Track Your Progress: View how many jokes are left to learn and how many you've mastered.
  • Interactive Animation: Enjoy a fun flip animation when revealing the punchline.

How It Works:

  1. Start the App: Open the app and browse a deck of dad jokes displayed on flashcards.
  2. Flip a Card: Click on a card to reveal the punchline. The front shows the joke setup, and the back reveals the punchline.
  3. Mark Known Jokes: If you know the punchline, click the "Mark as Known" button to remove it from the active deck.
  4. Move to the Next Joke: Click the Next button to move to the next joke.
  5. Track Your Progress: The progress bar shows how many jokes are left and how many you've mastered.
  6. Shuffling the Deck: Refresh the page to shuffle the jokes and start a new session.
